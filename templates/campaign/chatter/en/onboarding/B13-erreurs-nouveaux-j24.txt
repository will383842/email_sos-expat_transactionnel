We've worked with a lot of Chatters and we often see the same mistakes at the beginning. Make sure you're not making any of these:

Mistake 1: Sharing the link too early
You reply to someone and drop your link in the first message. Result: the person ignores your message or reports you as spam. Help first. The link comes after.

Mistake 2: Targeting groups that are too broad
A "travelers" group with 500,000 members is too vague. Go for specific groups. Search for terms like: "expats in Vietnam", "legal issues abroad", "expat jobs in the USA", "student visa refused"...

Mistake 3: Being inconsistent
1 hour on Monday, nothing for 10 days. That doesn't work. 20 minutes a day is 10 times better.

Mistake 4: Giving up too early
The first results usually come after 2 to 3 weeks of regular activity. Don't judge after 3 days.

Are you making any of these mistakes? Fix it today.
