After months of experience with our Chatters, we've identified the situations that convert best into SOS-Expat calls. Keep these in mind when you browse the groups:

LEGAL EMERGENCIES

  • Arrested or detained abroad
  • Accident with unresolved dispute
  • Eviction or urgent housing problem
  • Dispute with a foreign employer

BLOCKING ADMINISTRATIVE ISSUES

  • Visa refused or cancelled
  • Expired residence permit
  • Customs or border problem
  • Documents lost abroad

COMPLICATED TRAVEL SITUATIONS

  • Flight cancelled with refund denied
  • Lost luggage with no compensation
  • Illness or accident while on vacation
  • Scammed abroad

In all these situations, people need help NOW. That's where you step in.
